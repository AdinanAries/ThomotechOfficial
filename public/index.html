Products

Queue is one of the services that TheoMo Technologies is bringing to the retail and service providing business market. Queue is a website that also has a mobile application supported by Android and IOS operating systems. This service is designed to help small businesses that do not have the resources to create a website, mobile app and managed or maintain them. Businesses that will use this service are sorted when it comes to creating or scheduling appointments for their customers because Queue provides them with a platform that will be handling or setting up appointments for their customers. Queue is a platform where smaller business service providers such as barbershops, Primary care physician offices, spa and salon and many more can open an account for their businesses and customers will as well create an account as a customer or an individual. Customers will use this service for free however, businesses that use this service will use it this service at a price. On this platform, individuals can search for the services they need with the options to minimize their search to a specific location and type of service. The platform will then provide a list of businesses providing services based on the locations of the individual and the businesses on the platform. The benefit of using Queue is that individuals will be able to see when the business has an open slot and then book an appointment at any time even when the business is closed, cancel an appointment and also reschedule an appointment on the same platform. Another benefit of using Queue as an individual is because you will receive notifications and reminders of appointments. Some advantages to businesses that use Queue is that they can edit their availability time for appointment or service and also hold a slot for a specific customer. Businesses can also take payment for services provided via the Queue app. Also, Queue will help organize the daily routine of small businesses. Businesses can also communicate with their customers via the Queue. TheoMo Technologies does not provide services directly to individuals on Queue but rather to small businesses. Any unsatisfied services received from a business provider patronized through Queue must be resolved between the customer and business provider. We are not liable for any business service provider on our platform. To ensure that businesses on Queue are providing the best and quality services, we have made a provision where customers can rate businesses based on their experience. Businesses that go below two stars rating will be dropped from the platform.
