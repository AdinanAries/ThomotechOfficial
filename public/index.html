Products

With our team of software developers working round-the-clock to provide various Software-As-A-Service (SAAS) solutions to help improve businesses and individual lives, we will continue to release important products that help solve specific probelms in society and in business.

As at 2020, we are pleased to anounce the release of the Queue app to help small businesses manage appointments schedulling for their customers. Queue is a web app(website) which has been optimized for use on mobile devices with equal support on both Android and Apple devices. Businesses that will use this service are sorted when it comes to creating or scheduling appointments for their customers because Queue provides them with a platform that will be handling or setting up appointments for their customers.

The Queue platform provides customers a list of businesses providing services based on the location of the individual. The benefit of using Queue is that individuals will be able to see when the business has an open slot and then book an appointment at any time.

Queue also has a built in online payments feature that allows the businesses to accept upfront payments upon ordering their services on the Queue software.

It is important to note that Queue is only a SAAS solution intended to improve the lives the customers as well as help the businesses grow and to well organize its daily operations.

We don't hold any authority over any particular business who uses the Queue app, therefore, We are not liable for any business on the platform. To ensure that businesses on Queue are providing the best and quality services, we have made a provision where customers can rate businesses based on their experience.

Businesses that go below two stars rating will be dropped from the platform. This is however, not a measure to weed businesses out, but instead to encourage them to improve on the quality of their services which consequently helps them to attract more customers and increase their sales.

With us advocating commitments to the customer, our team of developers are still working hard to improve the Queue software as well as the other products we put into the targetted markets.


Important facts about Queue


Why Choose Queue for your business
We at Theomo tech understand how businesses have evolved over the years as technology has evolved. Queue web app is designed to help small businesses operate effectively, efficiently, improve services, expand their customer base, and increase revenues.

Why choose Queue as a customer
"Queue" is free for all customers. Customers can read reviews, comments, and view pictures from previous customers of business before scheduling an appointment. You can schedule or cancel an appointment at any time even when the business is not in operating hours.

How Queue operate for business
"Queue" is more than a scheduling app for businesses. "Queue" is a marketing tool for businesses to get their business out there to potential customers. One interesting thing about one of the features of Queue is posting pictures of the services they provided to customers. Businesses can use this feature with captivating captions like “picture of the week” or “hair cut of the week”. Any customer who searches for the type of service you provide will see the pictures you posted for that week and the pictures that most customers commented on. Therefore, your business is reaching potential customers and staying connected to your customers on the platform we provide. "Queue" will help you prepare for the day ahead of time. The customers that schedule the appointment also can select a specific style they want. Hence, the business knows ahead of time the number of customers they will be providing services to.

How Queue operate for customers
"Queue" is an appointment scheduling web app that will provide customers with the best service providers in their local areas. Customers can select a specific business based on the reviews of previous customers and ratings of businesses on our platform. We are dedicated to providing you with the best service providing businesses in our local areas. Under businesses that have below two-star rating will be dropped from our platform.
